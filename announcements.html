    Q. What does it mean "Website must have ... meaningful content"? Can we just fill it with Lorem Ipsum? Can we copy content from somewhere else?
    A. Lorem Ipsum is not meaningful content. Your Website should have at least 5 pages that a regular course website would have (e.g. Schedule, Instructors, etc.). The topic of the course is up to you. It can be a silly one or it can be a serious one. You can of course copy the content from online sources (I do not expect you to design a course). Just make sure to add a reference to the original source of the content.
