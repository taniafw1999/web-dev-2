Grading & grades

Grading

Three group-based practical assignments will cover 50% of the course grade. The other 50% will be covered by the two exams. The course grade is computed as follows:

  • Assignment 1: 10%
  • Assignment 2: 15%
  • Assignment 3: 25%
  • Midterm: 25%
  • Final: 25%

Retake

To pass the course you need to:

  • submit all assignments (hence, even if an assignment is not fully complete, do submit to get a non-zero grade for it);
  • get an average assignment grade of 4.0 or more;
  • take both exams;
  • get an average exam grade of 4.0 or more.

The retake exam can be used as a substitute for either the midterm or the final exam. If you miss one of the exams (AANV grade) or score too low on one of them and get a course grade between 4.0 and 5.5, you are eligible for a retake.

You cannot retake assignments.

If you want to get the best possible grade, treat the deadline for assignments as strict. If you miss a deadline, you have a day to submit it by e-mail, but you loose 25% of the grade.
